# 📊 Awesome Chocolates Business Dashboard

## Overview
This repository contains a performance analysis dashboard for **Awesome Chocolates**, providing insights into sales, shipments, profitability, and product performance across multiple geographies. The dashboard is designed to help stakeholders track KPIs, identify growth opportunities, and optimize decision-making.

---

## Key Metrics
- **Total Amount:** 61.59M  
- **Total Boxes:** 4M  
- **Shipment Count:** 11K  
- **Total Profit:** 35.21M  
- **Profit %:** 57.2%  
- **Date Range:** 01-04-2024 to 22-03-2025  

---

## Visualizations
- **Sales Trends:** Line charts comparing current year (CY) vs previous year (PY) for sales and shipments.  
- **Shipment Distribution:** Histogram showing shipment counts across box bins.  
- **Geographic Sales:** Pie chart showing revenue distribution across India, Australia, New Zealand, USA, Canada, and UK.  
- **Top Products:** Bar chart highlighting the six best-selling products.  
- **Top Salespersons:** Table ranking sales performance by revenue and boxes sold.  
- **Product Profitability:** Table showing profit margins and contribution for key products.  

---

## Highlights
- **Top Geography:** India leads with 27.55% of total sales.  
- **Best-Selling Product:** Organic Choco Syrup ($5.15M).  
- **Most Profitable Product:** Peanut Butter Cubes (90.2% profit margin).  
- **Top Salesperson:** Ponnan ($5.2M revenue, 316.5K boxes).  
- **Performance Concern:** Baker’s Choco Chips shows a negative profit margin (-3.0%).  

---

## Purpose
This dashboard enables:
- Monitoring of **sales growth** and **shipment efficiency**.  
- Identifying **high-performing products** and **salespersons**.  
- Spotting **profitability issues** for corrective action.  
- Supporting **data-driven decisions** for expansion and optimization.  

---

## Usage
- Open the dashboard to explore KPIs and visualizations.  
- Filter by date range to analyze performance trends.  
- Use product and salesperson tables to drill down into specific performance drivers.
